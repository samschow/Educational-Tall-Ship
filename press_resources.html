Partners

Call of the Sea is the premier provider of experiential education sailing programs in the Bay Area. Call of the Sea will operate the Educational Tall Ship as a sister ship to their schooner Seaward. The Tall Ship will enable Call of the Sea to expand their programs threefold. Together, Seaward and ETS, will be able to offer on-the-water experiences to more than 10,000 kids per year!
